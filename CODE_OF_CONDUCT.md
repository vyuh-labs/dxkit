# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

The `@vyuhlabs/dxkit` project is committed to providing a welcoming,
inclusive, and harassment-free experience for everyone who participates
in our community — regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible
disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and
expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status,
nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity
and orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open,
welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

## Standards

We adopt the [Contributor Covenant, version
2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/)
as the canonical text governing community standards, enforcement
responsibilities, and enforcement guidelines for this project. All
contributors, maintainers, and other participants are expected to
follow it.

In short:

- Be kind and respectful in all interactions.
- Welcome newcomers and assume good faith.
- Give and accept constructive feedback gracefully.
- Take responsibility for mistakes and learn from them.
- Prioritize what is best for the overall community.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all community spaces — GitHub
issues, pull requests, discussions, the project's npm presence, and
any other channels where someone is acting as a project participant.
It also applies when an individual is officially representing the
project in public spaces.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness behavior that violates this Code of
Conduct, please report it privately to the maintainers via GitHub's
[Private Vulnerability
Reporting](https://github.com/vyuh-labs/dxkit/security/advisories/new).
Although the channel is named "security," it routes directly to the
maintainers and is appropriate for any sensitive matter, including
Code-of-Conduct concerns.

Please include:

- A description of the incident.
- Where and when it occurred (issue / PR / discussion URL if applicable).
- Anyone else involved or who witnessed it.
- Whether you would like to remain anonymous to the reported party.

All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.
The maintainers are obligated to respect the privacy and security of
the reporter of any incident.

## Enforcement

The maintainers follow the [Contributor Covenant Enforcement
Guidelines](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/#enforcement-guidelines)
when determining the consequences of any action they deem in
violation of this Code of Conduct. Consequences range from a private
written warning to a permanent ban, depending on severity and
recurrence.

Maintainers who do not follow or enforce the Code of Conduct in good
faith may face temporary or permanent repercussions as determined by
other maintainers.
